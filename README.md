There are  people attending an event. Alice who is a programmer, also attending that event, becomes curious to figure out what is the social value of this group of people. To make things simpler, she assigns each person (including herself) a positive integer as their social status.  Alice knows that person A socializes with person B if and only if the absolute difference of the social status of A and B is less than or equal to a limit. According to this, each person has a social circle containing all people they can socialize with (including themselves).  Moreover, Alice believes that the sum of the sizes of the social circles of all individuals in the event, can be a good measure for the social value of this group of people. Since Alice is busy attending the event, she has asked you to find this number for her.
